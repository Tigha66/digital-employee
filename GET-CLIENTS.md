## Phase 2: Find Leads (Week 1-2)

### Step 4: Identify Your Target Market

**Best SMBs to target right now:**

| Industry | Why | Pain Point |
|----------|-----|-------------|
| Insurance agents | High lead volume | Can't follow up |
| Real estate agents | Busy showing houses | Miss leads |
| Medical practices | Staff overwhelmed | Administrative work |
| Financial advisors | Client communication | Time-consuming |
| Marketing agencies | Multiple clients | Administrative |

**Start with 1 industry. Master it. Then expand.**

### Step 5: Find Leads (Free Methods)

#### Method A: LinkedIn
1. Search: "insurance agent [your city]" or "real estate broker [your city]"
2. Look at profiles - find agents with 100+ connections
3. Send connection request:
> "Hi [Name], I help [industry] professionals save 40+ hours/week with AI employees. Would you be open to a 2-minute chat?"

#### Method B: Google/Local Search
1. Search: "insurance agency [city]" or "real estate [city]"
2. Note the businesses
3. Find their contact info

#### Method C: Industry Groups
1. Join Facebook/LinkedIn groups for your target industry
2. Introduce yourself, help people
3. Pitch in DMs later

## Phase 3: Convert (Week 2-4)

### Step 7: The Sales Call (15 min)

**Call Script:**

1. **Opening (2 min)**
> "Thanks for your time. Before we dive in, what's the #1 thing eating your time right now?"

2. **Discovery (5 min)**
Ask:
- "How many leads come in per week?"
- "What happens to them?"
- "What's your average client worth?"
- "How do you handle scheduling?"

3. **Demo (5 min)**
Show them the landing page. Point to:
- Email management capability
- Lead response speed
- Calendar integration
- Pricing

4. **Close (3 min)**
> "Here's what I can do: Deploy an AI employee that handles [their main pain point]. One-time setup $997, then $297/month. Want to move forward?"

### Step 8: Handle Objections

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|-----------|
| "Too expensive" | "What would it be worth to save 40 hours/month? That's like $8/hour vs your $200/hour value" |
| "Need to think" | "What specifically? Let's address it now" |
| "I have staff" | "This assists them - makes your team 10x more productive" |
| "Not interested" | "No problem. If anything changes, I'm here" |
