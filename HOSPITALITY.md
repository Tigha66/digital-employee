## What the AI Employee Handles

### 1. Guest Communication
- **Pre-arrival:** Confirmations, special requests, early check-in
- **During stay:** Room service, recommendations, complaints
- **Post-stay:** Check-out follow-ups, review requests

### 2. Booking Management
- **Inquiry response:** Respond to Booking.com, TripAdvisor, direct emails
- **Availability checking:** Real-time room status
- **Upselling:** Suggest upgrades, late checkout

### 3. Housekeeping Coordination
- **Room status:** Track clean/dirty/occupied
- **Requests:** Towels, toiletries, maintenance
- **Staff assignment:** Alert housekeeping of check-outs

### 4. Review Management
- **Monitor:** TripAdvisor, Google, Booking.com
- **Respond:** Professional responses within hours
- **Escalate:** Flag negative reviews to manager

### 5. Concierge Services
- **Restaurant reservations**
- **Transportation booking**
- **Local recommendations**
- **Event tickets**
