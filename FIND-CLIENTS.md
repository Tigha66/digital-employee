## Method 2: LinkedIn Outreach

### Step 1: Find Prospects

1. Go to LinkedIn
2. Search: "insurance agent" or "real estate agent"
3. Filter by your city
4. Look for people with 500+ connections (active)

### Step 2: Send Connection Request

```
Hi [Name], I help [industry] professionals save 20+ hours/week with AI employees. Would you be open to a quick chat?
```

### Step 3: Message After Connecting

```
Thanks for connecting, [Name]!

I help businesses like yours automate lead follow-up, email responses, and scheduling.

One client saved 35 hours/week and closed $21k more per month.

Worth a 5-minute call to see how it works?
```
